01 Who we are

Product4u is an independently operated digital products store. It is run by a single creator who designs, builds, and sells the templates, spreadsheets, and digital tools listed on this website. Throughout this policy, "we," "us," and "Product4u" refer to this store and its creator. "You" refers to anyone visiting or using this website.

This policy applies to all pages of this website, including the homepage, shop, product demo pages, and blog.

02 Information we collect

We collect information in a few limited ways, and only when you choose to provide it. We do not require account creation, and we do not track you across other websites.

  • Information you give us directly — such as your name and email address when you contact us to purchase a product, submit the feedback form, or subscribe for product launch notifications.
  • Feedback and reviews — if you use the feedback form on our homepage, we collect your name, email address, star rating, and written review.
  • Email correspondence — if you email us to make a purchase or ask a question, we retain that email thread to process your order and respond to you.
  • Automatically collected information — like most websites, our hosting provider and any analytics or advertising tools we use (see Section 5) may automatically collect general information such as browser type, device type, approximate location, and pages visited. This is standard, non-identifying technical data.

We do not collect payment card details directly. Payments are handled through external payment methods (PayPal, bank transfer, or card processors) that you interact with separately — we never see or store your full card or banking information.

03 How we use your information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes, and no others:

  • To process and deliver the digital products you purchase
  • To respond to your questions, support requests, or custom order enquiries
  • To send you product files and order confirmations
  • To notify you about new product launches, if you've subscribed to updates
  • To improve this website and our products, based on feedback you submit
  • To display reviews or testimonials, but only with your separate, explicit permission — we never publish a name or review without asking first

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes. Ever.

04 The feedback form and Google Sheets

Our homepage includes a short feedback form where visitors can share their name, email address, a star rating, and a written review of the site. When you submit this form, your responses are sent to a private Google Sheet that only the site owner can access.

Important: This Google Sheet is not public. It is not indexed by search engines, and it is not shared with any third party. It exists solely so the creator of Product4u can read your feedback and improve the website and products.

By submitting the feedback form, you consent to your name, email, rating, and review being stored in this manner. You may request deletion of this data at any time by emailing us — see Section 8 for details.

05 Cookies and advertising

This website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies, including those served through Google AdSense, to display advertising and measure site performance.

  • Google, as a third-party vendor, may use cookies to serve ads on this site based on your prior visits to this website or other websites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the internet.
  • You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings at adssettings.google.com, or by visiting aboutads.info if you wish to opt out of third-party vendors' use of cookies for personalised advertising.
  • We may also use basic analytics tools to understand which pages are visited most and how visitors navigate the site. This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Disabling cookies may affect how some parts of this site function.

07 How long we keep your data

We retain your information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy. Order and support email correspondence is generally kept for record-keeping and customer support purposes. Feedback form submissions are kept until you request deletion or until they are no longer useful for improving the site.

If you'd like your data deleted sooner, simply email us and we'll remove it within a reasonable timeframe.

08 Your rights and choices

Regardless of where you're located, we extend the following rights to every visitor:

  • Access — you can ask what information we hold about you
  • Correction — you can ask us to correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion — you can ask us to delete your information, including feedback form submissions
  • Opt-out — you can unsubscribe from product launch emails at any time by replying "unsubscribe" to any email from us
  • Objection — you can object to how your data is used by contacting us directly

To exercise any of these rights, simply email us — see Section 11. We aim to respond to all privacy requests within a reasonable timeframe, typically within a few business days.

09 Children's privacy

Product4u is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the relevant age of digital consent in your region), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10 Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as the site, products, or applicable laws change. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date at the top. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

11 Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, or want to exercise any of the rights listed above, reach out — we read and respond to every message personally.

Questions about your privacy?
